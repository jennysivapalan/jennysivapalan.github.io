Jenny Sivapalan

I am a software developer working at The Guardian. I tech lead a team developing the Content API which has helped the Guardian to expand on to many platforms. It's also been a useful tool for developers to hack together ideas too. It's an exciting organisation to work for and I have recently started a blog to document some of the things I have learnt.

Outside of work I can often be found on Clapham Common at BMF and in the summer co-captaining the Mob softball team . When I'm not doing that I'll probably be on my sofa with a cup of tea.

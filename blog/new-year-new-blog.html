New year, new blog

January 4, 2014

This is the year I start writing things down properly and the start of this blog. I’ve learnt a lot from some clever people over the years both in person and via blogs and sites such as stackoverflow. In the past I’ve used email or Pocket to share this with others or keep a handy reference for myself. This is fine until I actually need to search again for the information that is older than a month. I’m hoping this blog will change that and help to remind me of some the good and bad things I’ve tried over the years.
